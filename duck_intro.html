Making Process and Features

Beijing Roast Duck is a famous dish in Chinese cuisine. It is known for its crispy skin and tender meat. The making process involves marinating the duck with a mixture of spices and then roasting it in a special oven. The result is a delicious and flavorful dish that is enjoyed by many people around the world. Some of the key features of Beijing Roast Duck include its rich flavor, succulent meat, and beautiful presentation. It is often served with pancakes, scallions, and hoisin sauce. If you haven't tried Beijing Roast Duck yet, you're missing out on a culinary delight!
