About Beijing Roast Duck

Beijing Roast Duck, also known as Peking Duck, is a famous dish that originated in Beijing, China. It is made from a specially bred duck that is roasted until the skin is crispy and the meat is tender. The dish is typically served with thin pancakes, scallions, and hoisin sauce.
